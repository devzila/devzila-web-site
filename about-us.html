How We Work

We are team that’s flexible and versatile to offer truly a service well tailored for minutest details. Our process is structured such that we instantly adapt to the requirements of each client. And above all, our goal remains to make our client’s experience with Devzila a Delight.


Fixed Cost Model
  • The scope of a fixed cost model is very well defined
  • The project is straightforward with little or no external dependencies
  • The customers are billed on defined milestones and this model work best for well defined, small to mid-scale project
  • Any changes in scope are estimated separately and requires budget approval from the customer

Time Material Model
  • •The scope of Time Materiel Model is defined tentatively, and evolves as the process moves forward
  • The project can be complex with a lot of integrations and external dependencies
  • The customer is billed bi-weekly or monthly with supporting timesheet
  • This model works well for any size of project

Dedicate Hiring Model
  • Dedicated resources working from out Offshore Development Centre are assigned to the customer in a Dedicated Hiring Model.
  • Project management are task management are both handled by the customer directly.
  • The customer is billed a fixed price for each resource on a monthly basis
  • The Dedicated Hiring Model is best for companies with in house IT staff to manage and direct projects.
